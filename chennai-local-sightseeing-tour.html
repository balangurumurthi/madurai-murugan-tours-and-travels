Full-day itinerary

Your Chennai sightseeing day, hour by hour

We pick you up early and drop you back by sunset. The route is optimised to avoid Chennai traffic and maximise time at each stop. Itinerary is flexible — add or skip stops as you wish.

Morning · 6:00 AM

Marina Beach

Start your day with a sunrise walk along India's longest urban beach. Watch fishing boats return with the morning catch, climb the historic lighthouse for panoramic views of the coastline, and enjoy fresh filter coffee from the beach vendors. The cool morning breeze makes this the perfect first stop.

Morning · 7:30 AM

Kapaleeshwarar Temple, Mylapore

Drive to the heart of Mylapore for darshan at the magnificent Kapaleeshwarar Temple — a 7th-century Dravidian masterpiece dedicated to Lord Shiva. Admire the towering gopuram, the sacred tank, and the intricate stone carvings. Mylapore is also perfect for a quick South Indian breakfast at one of the legendary eateries nearby.

Mid-morning · 9:30 AM

Fort St. George

Visit India's first English fortress, built in 1644 by the British East India Company. Explore the Fort Museum housing rare colonial artefacts, portraits, and weaponry. Step inside St. Mary's Church — the oldest Anglican church in Asia. The fort complex also houses the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.

Mid-morning · 11:00 AM

Government Museum, Egmore

One of India's oldest museums, the Government Museum in Egmore houses an impressive collection of Bronze Gallery sculptures, archaeological finds from the Pallava and Chola dynasties, Roman antiquities, and a fascinating natural history section. Allow about 60-90 minutes to explore the galleries.

Afternoon · 1:00 PM

San Thome Cathedral

After a lunch break at a local restaurant (our driver will recommend the best spots), visit the stunning San Thome Cathedral — a neo-Gothic basilica built over the tomb of St. Thomas the Apostle. The white spires, stained-glass windows, and the underground tomb chapel make this a must-see landmark.

Afternoon · 3:00 PM

Valluvar Kottam

Pay homage at this grand monument dedicated to the Tamil poet-saint Thiruvalluvar. The stone chariot replica and the auditorium — one of Asia's largest — are impressive. All 1,330 couplets of the Thirukkural are inscribed on granite pillars here. A cultural landmark that every visitor to Chennai should see.

Evening · 4:30 PM

Birla Planetarium

Catch a sky theatre show at the B. M. Birla Planetarium — one of the most modern planetariums in India. The dome theatre screens shows about constellations, galaxies and space exploration. A wonderful experience for families and children. Check show timings in advance.

Evening · 5:30 PM

T. Nagar Shopping / Marina Beach Sunset

End your day with a choice: explore the bustling T. Nagar shopping district for silk sarees, jewellery and street food at Ranganathan Street — or return to Marina Beach for a spectacular sunset over the Bay of Bengal. Our driver drops you back at your hotel or any location in Chennai.

Chennai local sightseeing — FAQ

How much does a Chennai local sightseeing tour cost?
A full-day Chennai city sightseeing tour starts from ₹2,600 for an 8-hour / 80 km package with an Etios or Dzire (4 seater). Ertiga costs ₹3,200, Innova Crysta ₹4,000, and Innova Hycross ₹4,500. Price includes AC vehicle, fuel and experienced driver. Toll, parking and entry tickets are extra.
What places are covered in the one-day city tour?
Our full-day itinerary covers 8+ places: Marina Beach (sunrise & lighthouse), Kapaleeshwarar Temple (Mylapore), Fort St. George (museum & St. Mary's Church), Government Museum (Egmore), San Thome Cathedral, Valluvar Kottam, Birla Planetarium, and T. Nagar shopping or Marina Beach sunset. The itinerary is flexible — you can add or skip stops.
Can I customise the Chennai sightseeing itinerary?
Absolutely. The itinerary is fully flexible. You can skip or add stops like Santhome Beach, Parthasarathy Temple, Guindy National Park, VGP Marine Kingdom, or even a quick stop at Elliots Beach in Besant Nagar. Just tell your driver or mention it on WhatsApp when booking.
Is breakfast or lunch included in the tour price?
No, meals are not included. However, our drivers know the best local restaurants in Mylapore, Egmore and T. Nagar. They will recommend great South Indian breakfast spots (like Ratna Cafe or Saravana Bhavan) and lunch places based on your preference and budget.
Can I start the city tour from the airport or railway station?
Yes. We offer pickup from Chennai Airport (MAA), Chennai Central, Egmore or any hotel in the city. Share your arrival details on WhatsApp and our driver will be ready at the pickup point. Airport pickup is included in the package at no extra charge.
