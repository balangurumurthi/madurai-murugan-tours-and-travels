Best time to visit Chennai

Chennai is warm year-round. Here's when each season shines — and when to book early.

☀️

November – February

Cool, pleasant weather (22–30 °C). Peak tourist season. Best for sightseeing, temple tours and beach visits. Book 5–7 days ahead.

🌞

March – June

Hot and humid (30–40 °C). Good for early-morning temple visits and hill-station escapes (Yelagiri, Kodaikanal). Fewer crowds, better deals.

🌧️

July – October

Monsoon season. Light to heavy rain. Green landscapes, waterfalls at their best. Carry an umbrella. Good for Pondicherry surf season.

Chennai tour packages — FAQ

How much do Chennai tour packages cost?
Chennai local sightseeing starts from ₹2,600 for a full day. Mahabalipuram + Kanchipuram 2-day packages start from ₹4,500. Pondicherry round trips start from ₹6,500. All prices include AC vehicle and experienced driver. Toll, parking and driver bhatta are extra.
Do you operate from Chennai?
Yes. Madurai Murugan Tours and Travels operates across Tamil Nadu including Chennai. Our drivers are experienced with Chennai roads, ECR, OMR and all outstation routes from Chennai. We are expanding our Chennai operations with a dedicated fleet.
Which vehicles are available for Chennai tours?
We offer Ertiga (7 seater), Innova Crysta (7 seater), Innova Hycross (7 seater), Etios/Dzire (4 seater), Tempo Traveller (12 seater) and Force Urbania (18 seater). All vehicles are AC with experienced drivers.
Can I book a Chennai airport pickup or drop?
Yes. Chennai airport (MAA) pickup and drop is available. Share your flight details on WhatsApp and we'll time the pickup perfectly. We also cover Chennai Central and Egmore railway station transfers.
How far in advance should I book a Chennai tour?
For weekday city tours, 24 hours notice is usually sufficient. For weekend trips, Pondicherry, Tirupati, or multi-day packages, we recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance, especially during Pongal, Diwali and school holidays.
Is the Chennai to Pondicherry trip via ECR scenic?
Yes! The East Coast Road (ECR) from Chennai to Pondicherry is one of India's most scenic coastal drives — 160 km along the Bay of Bengal. We stop at Mahabalipuram, Crocodile Bank and Tiger Cave en route. Our drivers know the best photo spots and lunch stops.
What is included in the tour price?
Included: AC vehicle, trained driver, fuel for the agreed distance, and bottled water. Extra: toll gates, parking fees, inter-state permits (for Pondicherry/Tirupati), hotel accommodation (for multi-day packages) and driver bhatta of ₹500 per night halt.
Can you arrange hotel stays for multi-day Chennai packages?
Yes. We offer car + hotel combo packages for multi-day tours. We partner with trusted hotels in Pondicherry, Mahabalipuram, Tirupati and other destinations. Budget, mid-range and premium hotel options are available. WhatsApp us your preference.
