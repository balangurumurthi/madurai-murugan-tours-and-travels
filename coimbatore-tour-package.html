Coimbatore to Ooty cab — the Queen of Hill Stations

Ooty (Udhagamandalam) is the most popular getaway from Coimbatore. The drive climbs through 36 hairpin bends on the Mettupalayam Ghat road, opening into sprawling Nilgiri tea gardens, eucalyptus forests and a crisp, cool climate at 7,350 ft. Our Coimbatore to Ooty cab covers the round trip in a comfortable day.

86 km via Mettupalayam 2.5–3 hrs one way 10–22°C year round
01

Ooty Botanical Garden

55 acres of terraced lawns and rare flora. The annual Flower Show in May is iconic. Open year-round, ideal for families with kids.

Must visit ~1.5 hrs
02

Ooty Lake & boating

An artificial lake from 1824, surrounded by eucalyptus and pine. Pedal & motor boats available. Great photo stop.

Must visit ~1 hr
03

Nilgiri Mountain Railway (toy train)

UNESCO World Heritage steam railway from Mettupalayam to Ooty via Coonoor. Book the popular short section from Ooty to Coonoor in advance.

Must visit ~1.5 hrs
04

Doddabetta Peak

Highest point in the Nilgiris at 8,652 ft. Panoramic 360° view through telescopes. Just 10 km from Ooty town.

Must visit ~1 hr
05

Tea factory & tea museum

Watch the journey of tea from leaf to cup. Buy fresh single-estate teas. Free tasting. Many factories accept walk-ins.

Optional ~45 min
06

Pykara Lake & Pykara Falls

20 km from Ooty. Boating on the lake and a short walk to the cascading falls. Beautiful drive through shola forest. Best in monsoon.

Optional ~2 hrs

Coimbatore to Valparai cab — the Tea Paradise

Valparai is a pristine hill station deep in the Anamalai Hills, far quieter than Ooty. The drive from Coimbatore via Pollachi includes the famous 40 hairpin bends through the Anamalai Tiger Reserve. Expect Nilgiri Tahrs, lion-tailed macaques, elephants and waterfalls. Our Coimbatore to Valparai cab is the most comfortable way to experience this route.

110 km via Pollachi 3.5–4 hrs one way 40 hairpin bends
01

40 Hairpin bends through Anamalai

The legendary drive itself is the highlight. Numbered hairpins climb through dense rainforest. Stop for chai at Aliyar Dam viewpoint.

Must visit ~1 hr
02

Aliyar Reservoir & viewpoint

At the base of the ghat road. Boating, garden walks and a cool photo stop on the climb up. Great for families.

Must visit ~45 min
03

Loam's Viewpoint (9th hairpin)

Panoramic view of the plains, Aliyar reservoir and the lower hills. Often misty — arrive by mid-morning for clearer views.

Must visit ~20 min
04

Monkey Falls

Roadside waterfall before the climb. Crowded on weekends but the splash is refreshing. Worth a 15-minute stop.

Optional ~15 min
05

Sholayar Dam & tea estates

One of the deepest dams in Asia, surrounded by Sholayar tea estates. Lion-tailed macaque sightings are common along the road.

Must visit ~1 hr
06

Nirar Dam & Athirapally side trip

Extend your trip across the Kerala border to Athirapally falls (76 km from Valparai). A great 2-day add-on.

2-day add-on ~3 hrs

Coimbatore to Coonoor & Kotagiri cab — serene Nilgiri escapes

Coonoor and Kotagiri are quieter, more scenic alternatives to Ooty — perfect for travellers who want tea estates, colonial charm and panoramic viewpoints without the tourist crowds. Our Coimbatore to Coonoor cab covers both towns plus the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway ride.

70–80 km via Mettupalayam 2–2.5 hrs one way 12–25°C pleasant
01

Sim's Park

120-year-old botanical garden. Cooler & quieter than Ooty's Botanical Garden. Rare conifers and a small lake. Annual Fruit Show in May.

Must visit ~1.5 hrs
02

Dolphin's Nose viewpoint

A 1,000 ft cliff shaped like a dolphin's nose. Sweeping views of the Coonoor valley, Catherine Falls and the surrounding tea estates.

Must visit ~45 min
03

Nilgiri Mountain Railway — Coonoor to Ooty

The most popular section of the UNESCO toy train. Book this 1.5-hour heritage ride in advance. Cheaper to start from Coonoor station.

Must visit ~1.5 hrs
04

Kodanad Viewpoint (Kotagiri)

The highest point in Kotagiri, overlooking Moyar river and Mysore plateau. Famous for sunrise. 27 km from Coonoor.

Must visit ~1 hr
05

Catherine Falls

250 ft twin-cascade falls between Coonoor and Kotagiri. Short walk from the road. Spectacular in monsoon (June–September).

Optional ~45 min
06

Tea estate visit & tasting

Coonoor is the heart of Nilgiri tea. Visit Highfield, Tigerhill or any working tea estate for a guided walk and tasting.

Optional ~1.5 hrs

Frequently asked questions

Coimbatore tour package — FAQ

How far is Coimbatore railway station from Ooty?
Coimbatore Junction (CBE) is approximately 86 km from Ooty via Mettupalayam Ghat road — about 3 hours of driving. We pick you up directly from the station main entrance with a name placard. See our dedicated Coimbatore railway station to Ooty section above. The 1-day round-trip cab fare starts at ₹3,500.
Can you book Ooty toy train (Nilgiri Mountain Railway) tickets for us?
Yes, we can help book your Ooty toy train / Nilgiri Mountain Railway (NMR) tickets in advance — both the long Mettupalayam-Ooty steam route and the shorter Coonoor-Ooty section. Tickets sell out 60 days ahead in peak season (April–June, October–December). Share your travel dates on WhatsApp and we'll secure your seats with the IRCTC tatkal window or regular booking.
How far is Ooty from Coimbatore?
Ooty is approximately 86 km from Coimbatore via Mettupalayam Ghat road. The drive takes 2.5 to 3 hours including a tea stop. NH-181 connects Coimbatore to Mettupalayam and the Ghat road climbs through 36 hairpin bends to Udhagamandalam (Ooty).
How far is Valparai from Coimbatore?
Valparai is approximately 110 km from Coimbatore via Pollachi. The drive takes 3.5 to 4 hours and includes the famous 40 hairpin bends through the Anamalai Tiger Reserve. Wildlife sightings (Nilgiri Tahr, lion-tailed macaques, elephants) are common — please drive slowly and respect the wildlife.
How far is Coonoor from Coimbatore?
Coonoor is approximately 70 km from Coimbatore via Mettupalayam. The drive takes 2 to 2.5 hours. Coonoor is quieter than Ooty and ideal for tea estate visits, Sim's Park, Dolphin's Nose viewpoint and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway ride to Ooty.
Can I do Coimbatore airport (CJB) to Ooty in one day?
Yes. Coimbatore International Airport (CJB) to Ooty is 96 km / 3 hours by road. We pick you up at the airport arrivals with a name placard and drive directly to Ooty. You can also do the entire one-day Ooty trip and return to the airport for a late-night flight. See our CJB airport pickup section for full details.
What is the Coimbatore to Ooty cab fare?
For a 1-day round trip from Coimbatore to Ooty, our rates start at ₹3,500. The exact fare depends on the vehicle (Etios/Dzire, Ertiga, Innova Crysta or Tempo Traveller), date and pickup location. Toll, parking and driver bhatta are all included — no surprises. Click "Get Quote" on the booking form and we'll send the exact rate on WhatsApp within minutes.
Which hotel should I book in Coimbatore?
Three popular options in Coimbatore (Kovai): Welcomhotel by ITC Hotels at Race Course (ultra luxury), Radisson Blu Coimbatore on Avinashi Road (modern premium, close to CJB airport), and Vivanta Coimbatore at Gopalapuram (Taj group, business + leisure). See our curated hotels section above. Tell us your dates and budget on WhatsApp — we'll send specific options with current rates.
What is the best time to visit Coimbatore and the Nilgiris?
October to March is the most pleasant time for a Coimbatore + Nilgiris trip — cool weather, clear skies, blooming flowers in the Botanical Garden. The annual Ooty Flower Show in May is iconic but very crowded. Monsoon (June–August) is beautiful at Valparai but landslides can affect the Ghat roads — we'll guide you on conditions when you book.
Can the Coimbatore tour be combined with Mysore or Bangalore?
Yes. From Ooty, we can extend the tour to Bandipur & Mysore (~140 km via Gudalur) or onwards to Bangalore. Tell us your start and end city on WhatsApp and we'll plan a multi-day Karnataka + Tamil Nadu circuit. Popular combination: Coimbatore + Ooty + Mysore + Bangalore (4–5 days).
